Meet Our Team

Meet our dedicated team of legal professionals committed to delivering exceptional service.

Nicholson Okwori

Senior Partner

Nicholson Okwori is a seasoned legal practitioner with over 16 years of experience spanning telecommunications, real estate, corporate governance, compliance, and regulatory services. He is the Managing Partner at Anchor Partners, offering tailored legal and consultancy services across Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa. His past roles include key legal and compliance positions at Avanti Satellite Communications, Vodacom Business Africa (now Inq), and NatCom Development & Investment Ltd. Nicholson has negotiated and implemented over 500,000 complex contracts globally and played pivotal roles in humanitarian initiatives with CeCe Yara Foundation and JODED Foundation, including UN-Women's EVAWG campaign. He holds a Law degree from Benue State University and a Telecoms mini-MBA from Falconbury, UK. He is a member of NBA, CIN, and CRMI, and currently serves as a Non-Executive Director on multiple boards across various sectors. Nicholson is known for his solution-driven approach and passion for client success.

Mathew Ojonimi Onoja

Partner

Matthew Ojonimi Onoja is an innovative and passionate Attorney and Solicitor, highly effective at developing creative case strategies and persuasive briefs. He holds LL.B (Hons.) from the Benue State University, Makurdi (now Moses Adasu University, Makurdi) and a BL from the Nigeria Law School. He was called to the Nigerian Bar in the year 2011. He is a member of the Nigerian Bar Association (NBA) and has interests in the areas of Civil and Criminal Litigations, Corporate, Commercial and Property Law Practice.

Agada Sunday August

Administrative Manager

Agada Sunday August is a passionate Web Developer, skilled Social Media Marketer, and an aspiring Data Scientist with a strong academic foundation in Computer Science (BSc, University of Calabar, 2019-2024). With hands-on experience in administrative roles at Biodun Adeyemi & Associates (Law Firm) and DM Sheffield, he combines technical expertise with organizational efficiency and communication skills.
